## Engagement plan

- First 60 minutes post-publish: reply to every comment, open new DMs.
- Pin one strong comment per post.
- 10–15 min/day outbound: comment on 5–10 niche posts.
- Weekly: Mon plan + schedule; Wed review analytics; Fri clear DMs.
- DM script (template):
  > Hi [name], saw your follow. We help [audience] with [outcome].
  > Are you exploring this for [their use case]?
